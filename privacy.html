Lanscan privacy policy

Personal information transmitted by this app

None. Lanscan scans ports. It does not collect any information from you nor does it relay any personal information to our, or anybody else’s, servers.

Do we use cookies?

No. Lanscan does not maintain any user session information whatsoever.

Is that really it?

Yes. Lanscan is a port-scanning tool. Nothing else.
